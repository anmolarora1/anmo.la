About

I find joy in doing sports - running, lifting, cycling - on a regular basis, and occasionally adrenaline-rushing ones like skydiving. I like using podcasts as a medium of learning - distilling insights from the journeys and wisdom of world-class performers.

In the next decade, I wish to change course a bit. We'll see where things go, but they will certainly be different. :)
